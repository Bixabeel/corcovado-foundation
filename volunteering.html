Volunteer Opportunities

Choose the program that best fits your interests and availability. All positions include training and materials.

Conservation

Sea Turtle Protection

Participate in night patrols, nest protection, and hatchling release on our nesting beaches.

  • Duration: 2-12 weeks
  • Schedule: Night (6pm-6am)
  • Requires: Physical fitness
Restoration

Reforestation & Agriculture

Help in nurseries, planting native trees, and maintaining community agroforestry systems.

  • Duration: 1-8 weeks
  • Schedule: Daytime (7am-3pm)
  • Requires: Willingness for physical work
Education

Environmental Education

Support school workshops, create educational materials, and lead community awareness activities.

  • Duration: 4-12 weeks
  • Schedule: Flexible
  • Requires: Basic Spanish
Research

Ecological Monitoring

Collaborate in data collection, biodiversity monitoring, and information analysis for our scientific projects.

  • Duration: 8-24 weeks
  • Schedule: Variable
  • Requires: Scientific background
Park

National Park Support

Assist with trail maintenance, signage, visitor assistance, and support for park rangers.

  • Duration: 2-12 weeks
  • Schedule: Daytime
  • Requires: Spanish/English
Communication

Photography & Content

Document our work through photography, video, or creating content for social media and promotional materials.

  • Duration: 2-8 weeks
  • Schedule: Flexible
  • Requires: Portfolio

Volunteer Application

Tell us about yourself. We'll get back to you within 48 hours with next steps, available dates, and program details.

Need a faster way? Write to us directly at info@corcovadofoundation.org

What happens next?

  • Our team reviews your profile within 48 hours.
  • We confirm program availability for your dates.
  • You receive details about costs, accommodation, and logistics.
  • Once payment is confirmed, you receive the Volunteer Orientation Manual.

Please send a copy of your passport and travel insurance policy to zoraida@corcovadofoundation.org at least 2 weeks before your arrival.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need previous experience?

Not necessarily. All volunteers receive training at the beginning. However, some programs like research may require specific knowledge.

What is the cost to participate?

The experience includes accommodation, meals, and field support through a participation contribution.

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothing, rubber boots, rain gear, sunscreen, insect repellent, headlamp, and a willingness to learn. We'll send you a detailed list upon acceptance.

Is there a maximum age?

There is no maximum age as long as you have the necessary physical condition. We work with volunteers from 18 years old to seniors.

Have more questions?

Our team is ready to answer any questions about the volunteer program.
