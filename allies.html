Conservation is a collective effort.

We collaborate with local communities, international NGOs, academic institutions, and government agencies to amplify our impact in the Osa Peninsula.

Our Network

These organizations share our vision and actively contribute to the protection of one of the most biodiverse places on Earth.

Global Conservation Partner

Supporting our sea turtle conservation and environmental education programs through funding and technical expertise.

Local Community Association

Working hand-in-hand with the foundation to ensure conservation efforts directly benefit the families of the Osa Peninsula.

University Research Institute

Providing scientific backing, ecological monitoring, and internship opportunities for the next generation of conservationists.
