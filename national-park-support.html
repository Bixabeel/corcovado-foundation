Strengthen the park. Empower the rangers.

National Park Support is the foundation's commitment to ensuring Corcovado National Park has the resources, infrastructure, and trained personnel it needs to protect one of the most biodiverse places on Earth.

Donate to park support Volunteer here

Protecting protected areas

Our national park support programs work to strengthen park infrastructure, support ranger teams, and enhance visitor education.

Infrastructure Improvements

Building and maintaining trails, research stations, ranger posts, and visitor facilities that enable effective park management.

Ranger Support

Providing equipment, training, and resources to park rangers who patrol and protect the park's extraordinary biodiversity.

Visitor Education

Creating educational materials, guided tours, and interpretation centers that help visitors understand and appreciate the park's value.

Real Results, Tangible Change

Every trail maintained and every ranger supported contributes to the protection of Corcovado's extraordinary biodiversity.
